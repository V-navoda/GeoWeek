The research component of Geoweek 2021 is running under the banner of International Conference of Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka (ICSUSL) 2021, and will consist of four sessions.

International researchers and academics are being invited as plenary speakers for each of the four sessions, and there will be a number of keynote speakers invited by the organizing committee of the ICSUSL 2021. The FOG, under Geoweek 2021, will be collaborating with the international and local partners in the industry to hold workshops to enhance the knowledge of the user community.

Join with other events and get connected with round table/open forum discussion(s) and gain local and global knowledge in hot topics
