    The Geoweek 2021 will be held from 29 November - 3 December, 2021 at the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, Belihuloya, Sri Lanka.

    Objectives

    The purpose of GEOWEEK is to strengthen relationships between industry and academia in Geospatial science and applications, promote research in its discipline, and scientific exchange among researchers and scholars.
